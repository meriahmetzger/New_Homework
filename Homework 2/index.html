Apple Dumplings Recipe

About:

I made this recipe with my friend Hannah after we went apple picking! (mine did not look this ap'peal'ing, get it? it's an apple joke)

Directions

  1. Go to your local apple orcahrd and pick 6 apples! Any kind will do.
    • Core, but do not peel apples.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Roll out puff pastry square using flour and a rolling pin.
  4. Place each apple into a rolled out pastry square.
  5. Place brown sugar, butter, and apple pie spice in the hole made by the absent core.
  6. Close puff pastry around the top of the apple.
    • Use a small amount of water to seal dough if needed.
  7. Place covered apples in a 9x9 pan.
  8. Bring mixture of butter, sugar, vanilla, and water to a boil.
    • Reduce to a simmer for 7 minutes.
  9. Pour simmering mixture over the apples.
  10. Put the pan in the preheated oven, let back for one hour.
  11. Cool before serving with vanilla icecream.
